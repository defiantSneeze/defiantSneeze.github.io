My Personal Interests:

I'm completely fascinated by the lower level functionality of computers. The higher level stuff is interesting as well, but I prefer low level. However, my emphasis is going to be full-stack web development, but I might take Advanced High-Performance Computer Architecture as my elective because I'm so fascinated by how computers actually work.

Summertime and the outdoors are incredible, but I believe that having a coding job makes you appreciate it even more. When you spend all week in a dark room gazing into a virtual rectangle of despair, the weekends are just that much better. You embrace them more.

Family becomes more important to me with every passing year. I'm a full-time dad to a seven year old girl named Isabella. So I spend a lot of time watching kids movies, going to museums, and other childish things.
